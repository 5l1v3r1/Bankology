Stock Market

The stock market is a crazy place, and it is constantly changing. If you invest in a company that ends up crashing, then you can lose tons of money. However If you invest in a company that rockets then you can make tons of money. Because the stock market is so crazy, and stocks sometimes only crash for a day and come back up in value the next day, there are so many opportunities to invest. For a beginner one tip for investing in the stock market is to wait until the stock crashes, and the cost of a share is lower to invest, so that when it comes back up again you will have less money to pay off.
